Do you have a file that you would like to keep safe from prying eyes? If yes then you have come to the right place! Use this Website to encrypt your files with AES-256 encryption!

Features

Client-Side

You're sensitive files never leave your computer! Did you know that other encryption websites may upload your files to their servers to be encrypted?!

AES-256

AES stands for Advanced Encryption Standard. AES-256 uses an encryption key that is 256 bits long! A supercomputer could take billions of years to crack AES-256 encryption.

No Two files Are The Same!

A randomly generated IV (Initialization Vector) is used to add randomness to the start of your encryption process. As a result, no two encrypted files are alike.

Privacy

Any File Type
